The Characters of Noli Me Tangere

Crisostomo Ibarra
He is the main protagonist in Noli Me Tangere. He grew up in a wealthy family and has a childhood friend named Maria Clara. He's known as "Anak ng nasira kong kaibigan" told by Kapitan Tiago.
Padre Damaso
A priest who hates Crisostomo. Always mad for the smallest problems.
Maria Clara
A sweet childhood friend and fiance of Crisostomo Ibarra. But, her grandfather, Father Damaso, disagreed about the marriage.
Don Rafael Ibarra
Crisostomo Ibarra's father. Has a wife named Donya Pia. Known as th wealthiest man in San Diego
Kapitan Tiago
A business man in San Diego and the father of Maria Clara. Was the one who called Crisostomo "Anak ng nasira kong kaibigan".
